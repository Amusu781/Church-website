First Time?

What to Expect

Whether it's your first time in church or you're returning to faith, here's what you'll find when you walk through our doors.

01

Warm Welcome

Our ushers and members will receive you with open arms. You belong here from the moment you arrive.

02

Anointed Worship

Our choir and music team lead Spirit-filled praise and worship that ushers you into God's presence.

03

The Word of God

Every service features powerful, practical, Bible-based preaching under the anointing of the Holy Spirit.

04

Prayer Ministry

We believe in the power of prayer. Come with your needs — our prayer team is always ready to agree with you.

05

Friendly Community

We are a close-knit family. You'll leave not just blessed but connected to people who truly care.

06

Come With An Open Heart

Dress well. Be conscious of what you wear. For your body is the temple of the Holy Spirit. But more importantly, come with an open heart ready to receive from God.

Ready to Join Us?

Our doors are always open. Come experience God's covenant peace for yourself.
