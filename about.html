Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

The Covenant of Peace Evangelical Mission exists to preach the undiluted Gospel of Jesus Christ, make disciples of all nations, and demonstrate God's covenant faithfulness to every generation.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ in its fullness, raise an army of Spirit-filled believers, and impact our community in Ibeju-Lekki and beyond with the love, power, and peace of God.

Our Vision

To be a house of prayer, healing, and covenant for all peoples — a church where the broken are restored, the lost are found, and every family is established on the unshakeable Word of God.

What We Believe

Our Core Beliefs

Our faith is grounded in the Holy Scriptures and the foundational truths of the Christian faith.

01

The Holy Bible

We believe the Holy Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

02

The Trinity

We believe in one God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — co-equal and co-eternal.

03

Salvation by Grace

We believe salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone — by grace, not works.

04

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with evidence of speaking in tongues.

05

Divine Healing

We believe in the healing power of God available to all believers through faith and prayer.

06

The Second Coming

We believe in the imminent, personal, and glorious return of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Our Leadership

Meet Our Prophet

Anointed and called by God, our prophet leads with the Word, prayer, and a deep commitment to God's covenant people.

Prophet

Prophet Ojo
Samson Olumuyiwa

Prophet Ojo Samson Olumuyiwa is the founder and presiding prophet of The Covenant of Peace Evangelical Mission, Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos. He carries a strong prophetic and apostolic mandate to proclaim God's covenant promises to this generation.


Through his ministry, countless lives have been touched, families restored, and the sick healed by the power of the Holy Spirit. He remains steadfast in the calling God placed on his life — to build a house of peace, prayer, and power in Ibeju-Lekki and beyond.
